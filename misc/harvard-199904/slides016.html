A Brief History of MacPerl

  • Started as MPW tool in Summer 1991 by Matthias Neeracher

  • Version 4.0.2 in January 1992, sockets and DBM by the end of 1992

  • In 1993, started mailing list, got GUI, put on Apple Developer CD, version 4.1.0

  • Ported to PowerPC in 1994, perl 5 in 1995, tweaks and fixes in 1996

  • Major additions in Toolbox modules in 1997, book in 1998, version 5.2.0r4 / perl5.004
