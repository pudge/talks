AppleScript

  • MacPerl usually executes more quickly

  • If you know Perl, Perl development is mostly faster and easier. If not, AppleScript may be faster and easier. In any case, developing scripts with Apple events faster and easier in AppleScript.

  • Embed AppleScript with MacPerl::DoAppleScript built-in function*

  • Embed Perl in AppleScript with MacPerl's Do Script event*

  • Making AppleScript access easier with Mac::AppleEvents::Simple, Mac::OSA::Simple, macperlcat, Mac::Glue project
