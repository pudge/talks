Issue #4: Interprocess Communication ("Don't"s Part II)

  • system, exec, fork, pipe, ``, qx, open with pipe

  • Target process might not exist, or might be different

  • Don't open a pipe to sendmail. Use modules: Net::SMTP (from libnet), Mail::Send and Mail::Mailer (from MailTools)

  • Sys V IPC (msg*, sem*, shm*) may not exist on Unix machines
