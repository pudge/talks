Issue #1: Newlines (cont.)

  • Wrong!

  • The value of \n is determined by several factors

  • \n is a virtual symbol for logical newline

  • On most systems, \n == LF, on some Mac OS compilers, \n == CR

  • \r often mapped to LF when \n == CR, for symmetry

  • For IO, \n may be interpreted to CRLF on DOSish systems

  • use binmode for reading and writing binary files
