Interapplication Communication (IAC)

  • What Mac OS calls IPC

  • Apple events are a type of Mac OS IAC

  • Mac programs don't, generally, have STDIN, STDOUT, STDERR

  • Apple events correspond to what users can do with the program's GUI, and objects and data that the program has access to

  • AppleScript is a language designed specifically for Apple events

  • Apple events can be sent and received from many languages, not just AppleScript
