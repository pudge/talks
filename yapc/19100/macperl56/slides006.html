Compilers

  • Previously only used Metrowerks CodeWarrior compilers for MPW, with builds for 68K, CFM68K, PPC
  • CFM68K is for 68K Macs that use Code Fragment Manager (allows dynamic loading)
  • MWC compilers proprietary and expensive
  • Now use Apple and Metrowerks CodeWarrior, 68K and PPC 
    	+-------+----------+----------+
	|       |  68K     |  PPC     |
	+-------+----------+----------+
	| Apple |  SC      |  MrC     |
	+-------+----------+----------+
	| MWC   |  MWC68K  |  MWCPPC  |
	+-------+----------+----------+
  • Apple compilers are proprietary and freely available
  • Metrowerks compilers are essentially better
  • Apple compilers build larger (slower ???) binaries
