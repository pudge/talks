What is "portable"?

  • Portable code is code that runs without modification on a wide variety of platforms.

  • "Unix" is not one platform. Most of the platforms that run perl are Unix platforms.

  • Portability concerns often primarily center around the non-Unix platforms.

  • Unix platforms do differ in some significant ways.
