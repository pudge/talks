Issue #3: System Interaction (The Don'ts)

Don't:

  • delete, rename, unlink, tie, open, or otherwise mess with an open, tied, or otherwise occupied file. Close or untie it first.

  • count on a specific entry in %ENV, or whether or not its contents are persistent.

  • count on current directories / relative paths.

  • count on signals.

  • count on filename globbing.
